Legal Rules

Legal Compliance: Legal Compliance: It is crucial to understand and comply with local, state, and federal regulations to avoid penalties and ensure the safety and effectiveness of the solar panel installation.

Permitting Process: Obtaining the necessary permits, such as building permits and electrical permits, is a critical step in the installation process.

Domestic Content Requirement (DCR): The DCR policy aims to promote domestic manufacturing and reduce dependence on imported solar equipment.

Non-DCR Solar Panel Ban: Starting in 2026, there will be a ban on Non-DCR solar panels in certain categories of projects, focusing on promoting domestic manufacturing.

Solar Power (Development, Promotion and Mandatory Use) Act, 2017: This Act mandates the installation of solar photovoltaic modules on buildings to meet a part of their total electricity requirement.

Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) Compliance: Key solar components must comply with BIS standards to ensure safety and performance.
